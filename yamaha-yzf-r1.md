# Yamaha YZF-R1

## Overview

The Yamaha YZF-R1 is a 1000cc sport motorcycle designed for high performance on the track and on the street. It is known for its high-revving engine, sharp and nimble handling, and a sporty riding position.

## Engine and Things to Note

- Inline four-cylinder engine
- High rpm power delivery
- Emphasis on cornering and stability

## Street Use

On the street, the R1:

- Feels the most comfortable at high speeds compared to the other bikes
- Has the firmest suspension
- Puts the rider in a forward-leaning, sporty position

> This motorcycle is best suited for riders who want a focused sport bike and are comfortable with an aggressive riding position.
