# BMW S1000RR

[Back to home](README.md) | [Next: Honda CBR1000RR](honda-cbr1000rr.md)

## Overview

The BMW S1000RR is a 1000cc sport motorcycle designed with a strong focus on performance and technology. It is known for its electronics package, smooth power delivery, and stability at high speed.

## Engine and Things to Note

- Inline four-cylinder engine
- Broad and smooth power band
- Emphasis on electronics and adjustability

## Street Use

On the street, the S1000RR:

- Has different ride modes, wheelie control, etc
- Feels the most stable and comfortable at speed
- Still has a sport-oriented riding position

> This motorcycle suits riders who want a powerful sport bike with advanced electronic support and tuning options.
